At SD Electrical, we’re always looking for ways to maximize your home comfort and make the most of your budget, and as part of our commitment to positively affect the world around us and do our part for a greener tomorrow, we are committed to providing quality EV installations. We strongly advise working with a licensed electrician who is capable of installing a variety of EV charging stations in order to protect your house and your EV.

SD Electrical provides installation services for EV approved charging stations including all Tesla charging stations. Now that you have a charger, we can help you with the installation no matter the type! Whether it is a Tesla, Chevy Volt, Chevy Bolt, Volkswagen Golf, Nissan Leaf EV or another make, we can complete your EV charger installation.
